# Grit Scale

**Abbreviation:** Grit-O  
**Code:** `grit`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**License:** Published measure, available for non-commercial research use  

12-item scale measuring perseverance and passion for long-term goals

## Scale Summary

- **Items:** 12
- **Likert items:** 12 (5-point scale)
- **Dimensions:** 2
  - **Consistency of Interest (COI):** The tendency to maintain focus on long-term goals despite distractions
  - **Perseverance of Effort (POE):** The tendency to work hard and persist despite setbacks
- **Languages:** en

## Scoring

- **COI**: mean_coded (6 items)
  - Mean consistency of interest score (1-5 scale)
  - Reverse-coded: grit3, grit5, grit7, grit9, grit10, grit16
- **POE**: mean_coded (6 items)
  - Mean perseverance of effort score (1-5 scale)
- **GRIT_TOTAL**: mean_coded (12 items)
  - Overall grit score (1-5 scale)
  - Reverse-coded: grit3, grit5, grit7, grit9, grit10, grit16
