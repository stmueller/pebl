# System Usability Scale

**Abbreviation:** SUS  
**Code:** `SUS`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**License:** Free to use (no known copyright restrictions)  

10-item scale measuring perceived system usability. Traditional SUS score (0-100) = 25 * (mean_coded - 1).

## Scale Summary

- **Items:** 10
- **Likert items:** 10 (5-point scale)
- **Dimensions:** 1
  - **System Usability (SUS):** Overall perceived usability. Traditional SUS score (0-100) = 25 * (mean - 1).
- **Languages:** en

## Scoring

- **usability**: mean_coded (10 items)
  - Mean usability score (1-5 scale). Traditional SUS (0-100) = 25 * (mean - 1).
  - Reverse-coded: SUS02, SUS04, SUS06, SUS08, SUS10
