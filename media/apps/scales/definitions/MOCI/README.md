# Maudsley Obsessive-Compulsive Inventory

**Abbreviation:** MOCI  
**Code:** `MOCI`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**License:** Published measure, available for research use  

30-item true/false inventory measuring obsessive-compulsive symptoms across checking, cleaning, slowness, and doubting dimensions.

## Scale Summary

- **Items:** 30
- **Likert items:** 30 (2-point scale)
- **Dimensions:** 5
  - **TOTAL**
  - **CHCK (CHECK)**
  - **Slowness (SLW)**
  - **Doubt/Awareness (DBT)**
  - **Washing (WASH)**
- **Languages:** en

## Scoring

- **Checking**: sum_coded (9 items)
  - Reverse-coded: MOCI15, MOCI22
- **DOUBT**: sum_coded (7 items)
  - Reverse-coded: MOCI11
- **Slowness Repetition**: sum_coded (7 items)
  - Reverse-coded: MOCI16, MOCI2, MOCI23, MOCI25, MOCI29, MOCI8
- **Total Obsessive**: sum_coded (30 items)
  - Reverse-coded: MOCI11, MOCI13, MOCI15, MOCI16, MOCI17, MOCI19, MOCI21, MOCI22, MOCI23, MOCI24, MOCI25, MOCI27, MOCI28, MOCI5, MOCI9
- **Washing**: sum_coded (11 items)
  - Reverse-coded: MOCI13, MOCI17, MOCI19, MOCI21, MOCI24, MOCI27, MOCI5, MOCI9

## Citation

> Hodgson, R. J., & Rachman, S. (1977). Obsessional-compulsive complaints. Behaviour Research and Therapy, 15(5), 389-395.
