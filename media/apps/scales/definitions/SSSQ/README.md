# Short Stress State Questionnaire

**Abbreviation:** SSSQ  
**Code:** `SSSQ`  
**Version:** 1.0  
**License:** Published measure, available for non-commercial research use  

24-item questionnaire measuring transient stress states during task performance across three dimensions: engagement, distress, and worry.

## Scale Summary

- **Items:** 24
- **Likert items:** 24 (5-point scale)
- **Dimensions:** 3
  - **Engagement (E):** Motivation, energy, and task commitment
  - **Distress (D):** Negative emotional states and dissatisfaction
  - **Worry (W):** Self-focused attention, social concern, and rumination
- **Languages:** en

## Scoring

- **engagement**: mean_coded (8 items)
  - Mean engagement score (0-4 scale). Higher = greater task engagement.
- **distress**: mean_coded (8 items)
  - Mean distress score (0-4 scale). Higher = greater task-related distress.
- **worry**: mean_coded (8 items)
  - Mean worry score (0-4 scale). Higher = greater self-focused worry.
